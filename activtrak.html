The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Cited in only 29% of the queries where Google's AI answers.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO consistently places ActivTrak in the employee monitoring category. The brand positions itself as Work Intelligence. This is a meaningful gap: monitoring is a narrower, more surveillance-adjacent label. ActivTrak is placed alongside pure employee monitoring tools such as Teramind and Kickidler, which are security and insider-threat products rather than Work Intelligence platforms.
