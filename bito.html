THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Bito's organic traffic is concentrated in comparison and alternatives blog posts. These posts rank for AIO-triggering queries but are not confirmed as AIO citations. The product pages themselves generate almost no traffic. Only one page (docs.bito.ai/ai-code-reviews-in-git/overview) is confirmed cited in AIO.

02

AI misreads the category

The brand name 'Bito' is shared with the well-known Bitcoin ETF ticker BITO, creating a severe entity disambiguation barrier. This suppresses any AI Overview coverage of bito.ai and prevents accurate category placement in AI developer tools.
