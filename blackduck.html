THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

AIO triggers on nearly all category and brand queries (92.5% trigger rate). However, Black Duck is cited in only 3 of 37 AIO-present queries (8.1% inclusion rate). This is the primary performance gap. The brand ranks organically on high-volume AIO keywords but is not pulled into the AIO answer box.
