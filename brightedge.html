THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

AIO triggers on 77.5% of tracked keywords, but BrightEdge is cited in only 16.1% of those, indicating a major gap between organic footprint and AIO citation inclusion. Glossary and foundational SEO content drives the majority of citations; newer AI search and AEO product lines have zero AIO citation presence.

02

AI misreads the category

Partial. AIO correctly identifies BrightEdge as an enterprise SEO platform but fails to reflect the brand's newer AI search and AEO positioning. The company has repositioned heavily into AI search optimization, but AIO citations are dominated by older glossary and educational content, signaling AIO perceives BrightEdge as an SEO education resource rather than as an AI search platform.
