THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

eimmigration by Cerenade appears in AIO for the highest-volume category query (immigration software for attorneys, vol 1,505) but consistently ranked third behind Docketwise and LollyLaw. Main domain cerenade.com earns only 1 citation; subdomain get.eimmigration.com earns 9 combined citations.

02

AI misreads the category

Partially accurate. AIO correctly places Cerenade in immigration law software for the eimmigration product, but the broader Cerenade identity spanning legal practice management (eCMS) and government eForms is entirely missing. AIO has no awareness of Cerenade beyond one product line for one buyer persona (immigration attorneys). Two of three product lines are invisible.
