THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

ClickUp is mentioned in AIO answer text at high volume (3081 mentions via Google AI Overviews) but primarily as a brand reference in text rather than as a cited authority. clickup.com pages (54 total) rank 13th among cited domains by response volume. Third-party listicles and competitor domains (Monday, Atlassian, Asana, Wrike) each outrank ClickUp as an AIO source despite being competitors.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO places ClickUp in project management narrowly; brand positions itself as converged AI plus work management platform. The AI-first narrative is entirely absent from AIO perception.
