THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Coast is confirmed cited on only one query ('fuel card ein only') in one captured AIO response. Among 40 tracked keywords, AIO triggers on 17 queries but Coast is cited on only 5 of those (12.5% inclusion rate). The single confirmed citation is on the blog page coastpay.com/blog/fuel-cards-ein-only/, positioned second behind AtoB.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO sees Coast as a narrow EIN-only fuel card competitor. The broader expense management platform, fraud controls, vertical specializations, transparent pricing, and field-service-specific positioning are entirely invisible. This misalignment costs Coast citations on high-volume 'best fleet cards' and 'fleet expense management' queries where the full product suite is the relevant answer.
