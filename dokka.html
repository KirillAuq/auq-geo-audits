THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

DOKKA appears in only 4 of 28 AIO-triggered queries. Quality of placement is strong when cited, but citation rate is the primary constraint. DOKKA earns inclusion on competitor pricing queries (AvidXchange, Tipalti) but not on core category queries for AP automation or financial close.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO narrows DOKKA's identity to AP automation and invoice processing, missing the financial close module entirely. This represents 50% of the product portfolio being invisible to AIO. AIO positions DOKKA alongside Tipalti and AvidXchange (AP tools) rather than FloQast and BlackLine (close tools), creating category misalignment.
