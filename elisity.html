THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from 81% of AI-answer queries

Elisity appears in 5 of the 26 AIO-triggering keywords, meaning it is absent from 81% of queries where AIO fires. It surfaces most reliably in broad vendor-list queries, not in the specific microsegmentation or healthcare queries where it holds strong organic rank.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO groups Elisity with OT security pure-plays (Dragos, Claroty, Nozomi). Elisity's actual differentiation is agentless microsegmentation across ALL environments including IT, IoT, IoMT, and OT. This OT-heavy framing undersells the platform's breadth.

03

Owned pages are barely quoted

Only 2 of Elisity's pages are cited by AIO. Both are blog roundup posts. Core product and solution pages are uncited. Several high-traffic pages with AIO-triggering keywords are not pulling AIO citations. Score is 2 out of 10.
