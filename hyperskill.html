THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Hyperskill pages are cited for syntax-level Python tutorial queries. No course or career-path pages appear as cited sources. All six cited pages are Python method or syntax reference articles from the /university/python/ path.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO places Hyperskill in the reference-documentation category, not the structured-education category. This is a significant identity mismatch. AIO treats the platform as an anonymous documentation node alongside W3Schools and GeeksforGeeks, not alongside Coursera or Codecademy.
