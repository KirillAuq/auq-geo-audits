THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

All 449 AIO mentions map to Nucor Corp (NUE ticker), steel stocks, or unrelated consumer uses of the string 'nue'. Zero mentions reference nue.io or any of its products. The 45% AIO trigger rate reflects category queries where AIO fires but the brand is absent from every response.

02

AI misreads the category

Critical entity collision. AIO conflates nue.io with Nucor Corporation (public steel manufacturer, market cap ~56B), thenueco.com (consumer brand), and Nue Vodka. No recognition of nue.io as a SaaS software platform.
