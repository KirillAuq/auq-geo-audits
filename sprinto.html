THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Strong trigger environment with 38 of 40 tracked keywords generating AIO results. However, Sprinto is cited in only 6 of those 38 results, indicating presence without visibility on high-intent queries.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO perceives Sprinto as a compliance monitoring tool in the narrow compliance automation category. Brand self-describes as an Autonomous Trust Platform serving enterprise risk and compliance, with capabilities spanning TPRM, AI Governance, and Risk Intelligence. This 2-3 category misalignment means Sprinto is invisible on product-intent queries where buyers search for trust platform, risk intelligence, or AI governance solutions.
