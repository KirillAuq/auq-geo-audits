THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

AIO triggers on 65% of tracked keywords (mostly off-brand tutorial queries). When triggered, Storylane is cited in only 7.7% of those results. All 3 cited pages are off-brand tutorial content about third-party tools. Zero product, use-case, or category pages are cited. Storylane earns mentions in AIO but has zero on-brand citations and zero category presence.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO categorizes Storylane as a tutorial publisher rather than a demo platform. Direct competitors Navattic, Walnut, and Guideflow earn category citations while Storylane is absent from the interactive demo category.
