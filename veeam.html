Key observation
Five distinct competitive tiers emerged from AIO citation analysis: (1) Direct enterprise competitors (Acronis, Rubrik, Commvault) cited on high-intent category queries; (2) Emerging cloud-native challengers (HYCU) cited on SaaS backup queries; (3) Consumer/SMB alternatives (IDrive) cited on budget-conscious queries. Veeam's AIO presence does not yet establish clear competitive separation.
