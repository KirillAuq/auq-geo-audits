THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from 28% of AI-answer queries

Wynter is cited in 28 of the 39 AI answers we triggered, a 72% citation rate, with an AI Overview firing on every tracked query. It is absent on 7 queries, all in the broad B2B market and customer research vendor framings.

02

AI misreads the category

Wynter leads with messaging and market research for B2B, and AI gets the messaging half right, citing Wynter as a message-testing and landing-page tool. The market-research half is under-recognized. On B2B market research vendors and providers queries, AI cites full-service firms and panels, not Wynter.
