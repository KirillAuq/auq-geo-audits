THREE FINDINGS THAT MATTER
01

The brand is missing from most of AI-answer queries

Over 80% of observed AIO citations involve non-business entities (celebrities, baby names). Raw citation count of 7 is inflated by homonym-driven references. True business-relevant citations estimated at 1-2 keywords of the 40 tracked. AIO triggers on 60% of keywords but chooses other sources 91% of the time.

02

AI misreads the category

AIO treats Wynter as a content publisher and thought leader, not as a software product or research platform. This creates a critical gap: the product, its features, pricing, and use cases are completely absent from tool-recommendation and category queries.
